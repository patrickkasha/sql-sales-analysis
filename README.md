SQL Sales Analysis Project 

Project Overview

The project applies SQL to a sales dataset to reveal information concerning customer behavior, product performance and revenues.

The data is organized into various relational tables, and it resembles a real-life e-commerce system. Key business questions are answered with the help of SQL queries and help to make data-driven decisions.



Database Structure.

The project is based on four primary tables:

* **customers: stores customer data (name, country)
* orders- includes order transactions (date, total amount)
* products - product contents (type, cost)
* **order items, order items connect orders and products in quantities.



SQL Skills Demonstrated

SELECTION and WHERE filtering.
* INNER and LEFT JOIN.
* GROUP BY and ORDER BY.
Aggregation functions (COUNT, SUM)
* HAVING clause
Conditional aggregation (CASE WHEN)



Business Questions Answered

* What are the best customers in terms of overall expenditure?
* What counts of orders that have been made by each customer?
* What products do you earn the most money on?
* What is the average revenue/customer?
* What are the high-value (spending > 800) customers?
* What is the variation in revenue by product?
